#1. Key Points
Content Idea Generation
The workflow starts by generating content ideas or topics automatically.

#2. Information Extraction (AI Processing)
The Google Gemini model processes the idea and extracts relevant, structured content.

#3. Content Formatting & Preparation
The extracted information is refined into ready-to-post text (clean, readable format).

#4. Multi-Platform Publishing
The final content is automatically sent to platforms like Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn.
